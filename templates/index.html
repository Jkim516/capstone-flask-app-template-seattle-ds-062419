Bike N' Ride

Forecasting Demand for Metro Bikeshare in LA Downtown
Jungmo Kim - Data Scientist

Urban bikeshare systems are a wonderful way to get around a city. Bikeshare is a great transit option for both commuter and tourist. However, it can be a frustrating experience to arrive at the station and discover that there are no bikes available. The Bike N' Ride app provides not only live station status, but also forecasted number of bikes available next hour on each stations.
