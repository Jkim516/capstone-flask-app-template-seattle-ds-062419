Bike N' Ride

Forecasting Demand for Metro Bikeshare in LA Downtown
Data Scientist: Jungmo Kim

Urban bikeshare systems are a wonderful way to get around a city.
Bikeshare is a great transit option for both commuter and tourist.
However, it can be a frustrating experience to arrive at the
bike station discover that there are no bikes available.
The Bike N' Ride app provides not only live station status,
but also number of bikes available next hour on each stations.
